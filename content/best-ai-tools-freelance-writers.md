## How to Use AI Tools Effectively

### 1. Use AI for Drafting, Not Final Content
AI is excellent for generating first drafts, but always edit and add your unique voice.

### 2. Combine Multiple Tools
Different tools excel at different tasks. Use Jasper for blog posts, Copy.ai for social media.

### 3. Learn the Prompts
The quality of AI output depends on your input. Spend time learning effective prompting.

### 4. Maintain Your Voice
AI should augment your writing, not replace it. Your unique perspective is your USP.
